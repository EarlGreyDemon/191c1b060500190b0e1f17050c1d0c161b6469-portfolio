Interests/Hobbies

Outside of game development, I have numerous interests including: Non-Photorealistic Rendering, Films/Animation and modding games/ripping game assets.

My passion for Non-Photorealistic Rendering (NPR) really kicked off after the success of Spider-Man: into the Spider-Verse. Seeing the choices made to break out of the norm of 3D animation (like smears, halftone dot shading and animation on 3s/2s) and push it more toward a comic book aesthetic made it stand out amongst an ocean of other films.

My interest in film came from the numerous video essays on the internet about films. There are quite a few elements to consider when constructing one, such as the framing, colours and sound design. For me, 3D animation is a good way to access this, because I'm a student and I'm not about to go hire some actors and make a short live-action film, Blender is free after all.

As for modding games, I started off with games that have support custom assets, like: Minecraft's texture/resource packs and TF2's custom folder. Afterwards, I got my hands onto community modding tools and got interested in model ripping too, as the opens the opportunity for me to take a closer look at the art details that would usually go unseen, or I can animate with it.
