VTuber Model

2023

Engine used: Unity

Tools used: Blender, Krita, paint.net,

Close up of model's face
Model side-profile without jacket
Some of the detailing on the jacket

A face made using the included shapekeys

A anime-esque styled model rigged with the 52 shape keys present on iOS facetracking software. Also includes extra toggles for extra iris options, facial emotes, etc.

Fully self made/designed, during the making I learned a great deal about the importance of good topology, mapping UVs well and animating shape keys.
